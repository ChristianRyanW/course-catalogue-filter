Why we are the best

The University of Arkansas at Little Rock is a metropolitan research university that provides an accessible, quality education through flexible learning and unparalleled internship opportunities. At UA Little Rock, we prepare our more than 11,000 students to be innovators and responsible leaders in their fields.

Scholarships

Whether you’re new to the scholarship process or have a few semesters of experience, we’re here to help you understand the types of scholarships for which you can apply. There are hundreds of awards available, so it often pays (literally) to do your research.

Programs

Diverse array of outstanding programs, offered by six different departments – Construction Management and Civil and Construction Engineering, Computer Science, Earth Science, Engineering Technology, Information Science, and Systems Engineering.

Online

UA Little Rock Online offers more than 400 courses and dozens of accredited degree and certificate programs 100% online. UA Little Rock’s award-winning, full-time faculty continue to develop additional online programs each semester to create more possibilities for the future of online learning.

Research

UALR provides information, services, and support to members of the UA Little Rock community to enable them to compete successfully for outside funding to conduct scientific research

Academics

UA Little Rock students get to learn from the best professors around in classes that are as engaging as they are rigorous. Coursework blends critical thinking with real-world experience, providing a springboard for internships and later careers in such in-demand fields

Student Organizations

Learning doesn’t just take place in the classroom, and student organization membership is a way to become involved in your academic or professional field and to connect with other students while you have a great time.
