Why we are the best

EIT and departments within EIT offer numerous scholarship opportunities. Below are the renewal requirements for EIT Scholarship Enrollment. Follow the links above for scholarhsip appliactions.

EIT Scholarship Requirements:

  • Students must major in an undergraduate EIT program
  • Students must live on campus their freshman year
  • Students must have at least a 3.0 Cumulative Grade Point Average at the end of each Spring Semester
  • Students must complete 30 credit hours each academic year. (Excludes Freshman year where 27 credit hours is the requirement)
  • Students must complete 140 lab hours each semester their freshman year and 70 hours each semester their sophomore year. (Lab hours are simply hours spent in the computer lab completing assignments, studying, or participating in the free tutoring program)
  • Students must complete 2 freshman development events each semester. (Freshman development events are activities designed to promote involvement with the the college and campus community)
