Student Organizations


There is a wide variety of student organizations and clubs representing many fields of interest registered at the university. Highlighted Event: Research Expo

UA Little Rock is proud to be a doctoral research university where our students have the opportunity to research everything from Carbon-Based Nanomaterials to Cyber Security. The 2019 expo is part of the university’s Research and Creativity in the Rock event, which includes the expo as well as individual showcases and demonstrations within the university’s colleges. At the expo, more than 200 undergraduate and graduate students displayed projects spanning research in computer science/information science, creative work, economics, education, engineering/engineering technology/construction management, health science, humanities, interdisciplinary, life science, physical science, service work/professional application, social science, and social work.

A large number of EIT students displayed their research and competed for recognition in the 2012 Student Research and Creative Works Expo held Monday, April 16, at the Donaghey Student Center.
